Getting Started
Eligibility & Plan Details

The AMX 401(k) is administered through Principal Financial Group. Plan ID: 6-36521. Eligibility begins after 6 months of continuous employment at age 21+.

1

Eligibility Requirements

Age 21 and 6 months of consecutive employment (or credited with 1 year / 1,000 hours). Must be a full-time employee.

2

Plan Entry Dates

Enrollment begins on the 1st day of the quarter after you meet eligibility. Entry dates: January 1, April 1, July 1, October 1.

3

Deferral Types

Choose Pre-Tax contributions or Roth After-Tax contributions — or split between both. Deferral changes are allowed quarterly.

4

Rollovers Welcome

You can roll in prior 401(k)s and IRAs anytime. Consolidate your retirement savings in one place. Contact Principal to start a rollover.

Five Investor Profiles

Designed for cautious investors with shorter time horizons or high sensitivity to short-term losses. Focused on capital preservation with some inflation protection.

⚖️

Moderate Conservative

Score 18–41

Seeks modest capital appreciation and income. Moderate time horizon or slightly higher risk tolerance. Still focused on capital preservation with some growth.

🎯

Moderate

Score 42–61 · Most Common

Seeks relatively stable growth with lower income. Higher risk tolerance and longer time horizon. Steady growth while limiting fluctuation. AMX plan default.

📈

Moderate Aggressive

Score 62–83

High risk tolerance and longer time horizon. Little need for current income. Main objective is capital appreciation. Should tolerate moderate portfolio fluctuations.

🚀

Aggressive

Score 84–100

High risk tolerance AND long investment horizon. Maximum growth objective. Larger and more frequent portfolio fluctuations. Not for those near retirement.
