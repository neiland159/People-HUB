Getting Started
How to Enroll in Ease

All benefit-eligible employees use the Ease enrollment platform. Follow these 8 steps to complete your elections before the deadline.

1

Check Your Email

Look for a welcome email from noreply@ease.com. Click "Sign Up" to create your password. Your username is your work email.

2

Start Enrollment

Click "Start Enrollment" on your Ease dashboard. Enter and verify your personal information — fields marked * are required.

3

Add Dependents

Add any spouse or dependent children you want to cover. Children must be under age 26. Click "Add Dependent" for each one.

4

Elect or Waive & Submit

For each benefit, click ✓ to enroll or X to waive. Review your summary, complete missing info, sign documents, and submit.
