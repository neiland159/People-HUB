Structured Interview Scorecard

Complete immediately after each interview. Enter notes in ATS. Minimum 2 interviewers required per finalist. No prohibited questions.

INT
SOP 2.1 — Interviews: Use structured, pre-approved questions only. No prohibited questions (age, family status, religion, national origin). Record notes in the ATS immediately after each interview. No screening based on name, address, or graduation year.
Interview Details
Competency Scoring
1. Role-Specific Knowledge & Technical Competency
"Walk me through how you've approached [key job function] in a previous role. What was the outcome?"
2. Problem-Solving & Critical Thinking
"Describe a time when you faced a significant challenge or obstacle in your work. How did you analyze the situation and what steps did you take?"
3. Collaboration & Communication
"Tell me about a time you worked with a cross-functional team or a difficult stakeholder. How did you manage the relationship and what was the outcome?"
4. Accountability & Results Orientation
"Give me an example of a goal or project where you fell short. What happened, and what did you do about it?"
5. Cultural & Values Alignment
"What kind of work environment brings out your best performance? What do you look for in a company's culture?"
Overall Assessment
Prohibited Topics Reminder: Do not ask or record observations about age, family status, marital status, religion, national origin, disability, pregnancy, or any other protected characteristic. If the candidate volunteered this information, do not document it.
